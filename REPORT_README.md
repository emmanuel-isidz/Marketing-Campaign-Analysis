# 📖 Project Overview

Marketing teams invest significant budgets into campaigns across multiple channels, but understanding which campaigns actually drive conversions and revenue is critical for maximizing return on investment.

This project analyzes customer interactions, purchases, campaign performance, and customer demographics to uncover actionable insights that can improve marketing effectiveness and profitability.

The analysis focuses on answering key business questions such as:

- Which marketing campaigns generate the highest revenue?
- Which channels have the highest conversion rates?
- What customer segments drive the most revenue?
- Are marketing campaigns generating positive ROI?
- How does customer behavior vary across demographics?

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The company runs multiple marketing campaigns across different channels but lacks visibility into:

- Campaign profitability
- Customer conversion behavior
- Marketing efficiency
- Audience segmentation
- Revenue attribution

Without proper analysis, marketing budgets may be allocated to underperforming campaigns, resulting in wasted spend and missed revenue opportunities.

The goal of this project is to evaluate campaign effectiveness and provide recommendations for optimizing marketing investments.

# 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The analysis focused on the following marketing metrics:

### Conversion Rate

Measures the percentage of customers who interacted with a campaign and subsequently made a purchase.

```sql
Conversion Rate =
(Total Conversions / Total Interactions) × 100
```

---

### Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)

Measures how much it costs to acquire a customer.

```sql
CPA =
Campaign Cost / Number of Buyers
```

---

### Return on Investment (ROI)

Measures campaign profitability.

```sql
ROI =
((Revenue - Campaign Cost) / Campaign Cost) × 100
```

## 2️⃣ Campaign Engagement Analysis

Measured total interactions generated by each campaign.

### Objective

Identify which campaigns generate the highest customer engagement.

### Business Value

Helps determine:

- Audience reach
- Campaign awareness
- Customer engagement levels

---

## 3️⃣ Revenue Analysis

Calculated total revenue generated by each campaign.

### Objective

Determine which campaigns contribute most to sales performance.

### Key Insight

Some campaigns generated significantly higher revenue despite having similar interaction levels.

This suggests differences in audience quality and campaign effectiveness.

---

## 4️⃣ Conversion Rate Analysis

Measured how effectively each campaign converts engaged customers into buyers.

### Formula

```text
Conversions ÷ Interactions
```

### Key Finding

📌 **CAM01 (Email Campaign) achieved approximately 40% conversion rate**, nearly double the conversion rate of other campaigns.

### Business Impact

Email marketing demonstrated significantly higher effectiveness compared to alternative channels.

---

## 5️⃣ Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)

Calculated customer acquisition costs across campaigns.

### Objective

Evaluate marketing efficiency.

### Business Value

Lower CPA indicates:

- More efficient campaigns
- Better customer targeting
- Stronger marketing performance

---

## 6️⃣ Return on Investment (ROI)

Compared campaign revenue against campaign costs.

### Key Finding

📌 Most campaigns generated extremely low ROI, indicating marketing spend is not producing sufficient returns.

### Business Impact

Current campaign strategies may require optimization to improve profitability.
